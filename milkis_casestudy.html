About the drink

Where soda gets its fizz, and milk gets its cool.

Milkis is a carbonated milk beverage that shouldn't work — and somehow works perfectly. Underneath the fizz is a smooth, creamy body that softens every bubble into something closer to dessert than soda.

First bottled in South Korea, Milkis has become the drink people reach for when a regular soda feels too sharp and a glass of milk feels too plain. It's the in-between everyone didn't know they wanted.
